Instructions

Your task in this study is to improve your proficiency at playing an obstacle avoidance game using your dominant hand. In the game, you are tasked with navigating a penguin across an Arctic course to its home through a series of obstacles. A feature of this game is that it includes an algorithm that can provide assistance to you as you play. Specifically, the algorithm will scale up or down your input to more closely match how an expert would play the game.

Instructions

To start the game, move the cursor to the red box (Starting point). When ready, click and hold the left button on the mouse to begin moving the penguin across the Arctic course.

You will play the game 11 times. The first game you play will be a practice round to become familiar with the game dynamics. During this practice round,you will not have the option of selecting to receive assistance from the game's assistance algorithm. The option of doing so will be available in games 1-10.
Each time you complete the game, press next and you will be re-directed to play again.
